The Town of Preston: A Hidden Gem

By: Katie Perry

Preston Idaho is a small little town found in Franklin County Idaho. It is predominantly a little farming community that not only grows great produce, but great people. In fact, my Father in Law was born in Preston Idaho. And of course, the legendary Napoleon Dynamite came from Preston Idaho as well. Between the fresh farm air and the fresh produce, Preston generates great people.
