Visual Hierarchy

Colegio de Ingenieros del Peru

www.cip.org.pe

Without knowing anything about this website, you could classify them easily. In this example you can see the hierarchy of the image of the engineering school that occupies the entire screen, followed by the logo of the school. That is a visual hierarchy.

Hick's Law

Computrabajo DGNET LTD

www.computrabajo.com.pe

Hick’s Law says that with every additional choice increases the time required to take a decision. This website is about job offers which has many options but this uses filters, so one can search by category, place or salary. Filters help you find something specific in a short time.

White Space and Clean Design

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

www.churchofjesuschrist.org/

The right amount of white space makes it website look “clean.” While a clean design is crucial to communicate a clear message, it doesn’t just mean less content, on the contrary this website has enough content. We can see white space in the space surrounding the logo, in the search button, as well as the space that separates each image.
