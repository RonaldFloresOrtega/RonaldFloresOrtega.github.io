Fish Haven: A haven for fish?

By: Katie Perry

Fish Haven is a small little town in Idaho. You may wonder if it is indeed a haven for fish? Or is it more a haven for fishermen? The latter is true. This quaint town near Bear Lake, a fisherman's dream, and is named after its original Post Office, Fish Haven, that was in operation until 1962.
