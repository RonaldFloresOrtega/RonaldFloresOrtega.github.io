Color Scheme

The color scheme I will be employing for this page will be on the “blues” and “yellows” portion of the spectrum. These colors I feel like would represent a weather site well (i.e. sun is “yellow”, sky is “blue” etc.). They are also earthy and natural tones which is very indicative of the Northwest.

The colors used are complementary colors which are always aesthetically appealing to users. The colors are calming which helps to improve the users experience.

Application

The colors used will be as follows:

#264653 Dark Teal as Headers and Footers.
#2a9daf Aqua as Navigation Bar and Header 2.
#E9c46A Golden Yellow as Accent and Borders.
#000000 Black as Titles and Text.
#e76f51 Orange as Hover Color on Menu.
