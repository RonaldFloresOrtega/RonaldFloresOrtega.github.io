Our Purpose

The purpose here at the Columbia River Report is to present accurate and area specific information for those here in the Northwest Columbia Basin area.

We are here with weekly weather forecasts as well as real time weather related road condition reports for those traveling through the Columbia River gorge and nearby mountain passes.

As Northwesterners ourselves, we are here to provide the information we know is essential to those living in this region.
