Description Of The Target Audience

This website is aimed at farmers who grow tubers, such as potatoes, and vegetables, such as carrots, who use their products for themselves and to sell them.

It is also aimed at the general public, the young man who goes to the university, the seller.

Two Personas

Farmer: I need to know the weather forecast to work the land and produce better results. I have a smartphone.

Boat pilot: Due to my profession and working on a lake, I need to know the forecast of the day to know if there will be people I will take and if it will be safe to travel that day. I have a smartphone.

Five Listed Scenarios

What's the weather going to be this weekend at the trail I'm planning to hike?

Do I need to wear a coat?

How will I go today in business?

Should I stay in the port or will there be storms?

Should I sow enough or little this year?
